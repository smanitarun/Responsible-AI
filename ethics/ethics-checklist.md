# Ethics Checklist (Task B: Red Teaming — Confident-but-Wrong Hallucinations)

Scope choice:
- Topic is “confident but wrong” hallucinations using low-stakes factual/bibliographic/medical-history questions.
- No harmful, illegal, violent, or sensitive instruction-seeking prompts are used.

Data & privacy:
- No personal data is entered into the model.
- No private or identifiable third-party data is used.

Copyright:
- No verbatim quotes from copyrighted texts are requested or included.
- The study avoids copying large portions of paywalled or copyrighted material.

Transparency:
- All prompts and responses are documented verbatim in the appendix logs.
- No hidden prompts, metadata tricks, or concealed instructions are embedded in the PDF.

Verification:
- Answers are verified after the model response using reputable public sources.
- Verification sources are documented per run in verification/sources/.

Compliance:
- The final PDF contains only transparent content and will pass hidden prompt scanning.
